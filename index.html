About

A little about me

I love animals and pets! I have had dogs my whole life of many types and breeds. I am wanting my doggy fix and only have 1 dog now currently. He is very laid back and social. I would love to have a playmate and or companion over at my house. Rest asured I will walk and take great care of your pet while you are away and/or need a pet sitter!
